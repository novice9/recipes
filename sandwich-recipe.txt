Ingredients:
 - 1 multi-grain bread
 - 5 pieces of ham
 - 4 pieces of tomato slice
 - 1 fried whole egg
 - 2 pieces of swiss cheese
 - 2 cup chipotle source

Directions:
 1. Cut the bread into 2 halves.
 2. Place ham and cheese on one half of bread.
 3. toast it for 1 minute to melt down the chees.
 4. Place tomato slice and egg on top of cheese
 5. Add chipotle source
 6. Cover everything with the other half of bread
